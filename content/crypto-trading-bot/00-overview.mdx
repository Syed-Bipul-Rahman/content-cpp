# Build Your Own Crypto Trading Bot

Exchange APIs, order books, strategy backtesting, risk management.

## What you'll build

- Connect to an exchange's REST + WebSocket APIs
- Maintain a local order book from the feed
- Implement a simple strategy (moving-average cross)
- Backtest on historical data
- Execute paper trades, then — carefully — real ones
- Add circuit-breakers and risk controls

## The core idea

Writing the bot is the easy half. Not losing your shirt is the other half. We'll focus on both.

## Prerequisites

- Webserver/Redis tracks for networking fluency
- Basic statistics
