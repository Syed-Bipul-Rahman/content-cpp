# Build Your Own FB Messenger AI Bot

Graph API, webhooks, and a small NLP integration.

## What you'll build

- Register a Facebook app and page
- Expose a webhook endpoint
- Respond to message events
- Integrate a language model for responses
- Persist conversation state

## The core idea

Messenger bots are the blueprint for enterprise chatbots. The tricky parts are verification, state, and not saying something dumb at scale.
